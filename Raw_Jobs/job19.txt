We are a fast-growing company building products in the fintech, gaming, and consumer technology industries. Our team currently operates a cashless gaming platform and is preparing to launch a sports gaming fintech platform. We are actively enhancing existing products while building new technology and working with enterprise and white-label partners. We are looking for a Founding Engineer who wants to help build products from the ground floor, move quickly, and play a major role in shaping the future of our technology stack.

About The Role

This is not a corporate engineering position. We are looking for someone who enjoys building. You will work directly with our development team and leadership to help accelerate development, improve product quality, and support the growth of multiple technology platforms. Initially, this role will be highly hands-on and focused on shipping product. Over time, this person will have the opportunity to help shape our engineering team as the company grows.
